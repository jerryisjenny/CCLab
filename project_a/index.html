[ FERRABLOOM ]

Scientific Name:

FERRABLOOM

Discovered by / at:

Jenny Tan, 10/25/1780

Family:

It has no family, or rather, the entire family consists only of itself.

Habitat:

factories, power plants, and automated production facilities

Appearance:

The creature stands tall with a smooth, metallic stem that has a soft sheen. At the top, it blooms with a soft flower that appear to glow faintly, each petal crafted with precision, reflecting light in a soothing way. The flower exude an ethereal quality, its edges rounded and inviting, creating a stark contrast to their industrial roots. When the fruit forms, it resembles a soft, spherical orb, its surface dimpled like a soft sponge.

Key Characteristics:

  • Grows in the dark.
  • Blooms in industrial wastewater.
  • ...

What we know so far about Ferrabloom? It is a product of industrial capitalism. Born from money and the operation of machines, it is nourished by human labor and ultimately blossoms. Unlike heavy metals, it is incredibly gentle. It flowers, bears fruit, and after the fruit decays and explodes, it returns once again to the dazzling money and roaring machines. It perishes, yet it will be reborn.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Try using the mouse to insert coins into the coin slot. After the machine operates, attempt to click on the center of each gear; something will appear. Try clicking with the mouse to encourage the Ferrabloom to blossom and bear fruit.
